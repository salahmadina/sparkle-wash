About SparkleWash

Making car care simple, fast, and convenient

Our Story

It all started with a simple problem nobody enjoys spending their weekend washing their car.

In 2018, a small team of friends noticed that people were tired of long waits, expensive services, and cars that never came back quite right. So SparkleWash was born.

We started with one small station and a big dream — to give people their time back. Today, SparkleWash serves hundreds of families and businesses across Egypt.

Our Mission

To make car washing fast, easy, and at your convenience. We are committed to providing high-quality, affordable car wash services that fit into your busy life.
