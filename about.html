About SparkleWash

Making car care simple, fast, and convenient

Our Story

It all started with a simple problem — nobody enjoys spending their weekend washing clothes.

In 2018, a small team of friends in Alexandria noticed that people were tired of long laundry days, expensive dry cleaners, and clothes that never came back quite right. They believed there had to be a better way.

So SparkleWash was born.

We started with one small laundry unit and a big dream — to give people their time back. Our first customers were students and busy professionals who needed clean clothes without the hassle. Word spread quickly, and what began as a small operation grew into something much bigger.

Today, SparkleWash serves hundreds of families and businesses across Egypt. We combine modern washing technology with careful attention to every single garment. From delicate fabrics to heavy-duty workwear, we treat every item like it belongs to someone we care about — because it does.

We are not just a laundry service. We are the extra hour you get to spend with your family. We are the clean shirt that gives you confidence on an important day. We are the peace of mind that comes from knowing your clothes are in good hands.

Our Mission

At SparkleWash, our mission is simple: to make laundry day a thing of the past. We want to give people their time back, so they can focus on what really matters. We are committed to providing high-quality, affordable laundry services that fit into your busy life. Whether you need a quick wash or a deep clean, we are here to help.
